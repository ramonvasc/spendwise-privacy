# SpendWise
## Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: January 2, 2026_

> **Privacy First:** SpendWise is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information or financial data on our servers.

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to SpendWise ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. By using SpendWise, you agree to the practices described in this policy. SpendWise is a personal finance and expense tracking application that operates entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your financial information remains secure and private.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect
SpendWise does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following information:
- **Financial Data:** Transaction amounts, categories, budgets, or any financial information you enter  
- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information  
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates or location-based information  
- **Device Information:** Device ID, IP address, or hardware specifications  
- **Usage Analytics:** How you use the app, which features you access, or usage patterns  
- **Photos and Receipts:** Receipt photos you attach to transactions remain on your device only  
- **Account Credentials:** No user accounts, passwords, or authentication data  

## 3. How Your Data is Stored
All data you enter into SpendWise is stored locally on your device using secure device storage mechanisms:
- **Local Storage:** Transaction data, categories, budgets, and settings are stored in your device's local database  
- **Photo Storage:** Receipt photos are saved in your device's local file system  
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your data is never uploaded to cloud servers or transmitted over the internet  
- **Device-Only Access:** Only you have access to your financial data through the app on your device  

> **Important:** Because all data is stored locally, if you uninstall the app or lose your device, your data cannot be recovered. We recommend regularly backing up your device if you want to preserve your financial records.

## 4. Permissions We Request
SpendWise may request the following device permissions to provide full functionality:

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To allow you to take photos of receipts directly within the app.  
- **Usage:** Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.  
- **Optional:** You can use the app without granting camera permission.  

### Photo Library Permission
- **Purpose:** To allow you to attach existing photos from your device to transactions.  
- **Usage:** Selected photos are copied to the app's local storage and remain on your device.  
- **Optional:** You can use the app without granting photo library access.  

### Notifications Permission
- **Purpose:** To send you local reminders about budgets and recurring transactions.  
- **Usage:** All notifications are generated locally on your device; no data is sent externally.  
- **Optional:** You can disable notifications in your device settings.  

## 5. Data Security
We take data security seriously, even though we don't collect your data:
- **Local Encryption:** Your device's operating system provides encryption for locally stored data  
- **No Network Transmission:** Your financial data never leaves your device  
- **No Third-Party Access:** We do not share data with advertisers, analytics services, or any third parties  
- **No User Accounts:** No login credentials means no risk of account breaches  

## 6. Third-Party Services
SpendWise does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The app operates completely offline and independently.

## 7. Children's Privacy
SpendWise does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, the app can be safely used by individuals of all ages under parental supervision.

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion
- **Retention:** Your data remains on your device for as long as you keep the app installed.  
- **Deletion:** You can delete individual transactions, categories, or all data within the app's settings. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data stored by the app.  

## 9. Your Rights
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:
- **Access:** You can view all your data within the app at any time  
- **Modification:** You can edit or update any information you've entered  
- **Deletion:** You can delete specific entries or clear all data  
- **Export:** You can export your data to CSV or PDF format for backup purposes  
- **Portability:** Exported data can be imported into other applications  

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, if appropriate, through an in-app notification. Your continued use of SpendWise after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## 11. International Users
SpendWise can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transfer any information, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your data remains in your physical possession at all times.
